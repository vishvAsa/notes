title = "Kagame effect"
+++

Source: [TW](https://x.com/blog_supplement/status/2060174782679642167)

The ethnic composition of Rwanda and Burundi is nearly the same mix of Hutus, Tutsis and Twas – all Bantu peoples. They are geographically and natural-resources-wise more or less the same. Hence, we can call it the king Kagame effect.


| Metric                  | Rwanda                                      | Burundi                                      |
|-------------------------|---------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| **GDP (Nominal)**       | ~$14.3 Billion                              | ~$3.08 Billion                               |
| **GDP per Capita**      | ~$1,000                                     | ~$219                                        |
| **Global Wealth Ranking** | Actively emerging tech/financial hub       | Consistently ranked the poorest country on Earth |
| **Economic Strategy**   | Service, tourism, tech, FDI-driven          | Subsistence agriculture, foreign aid-dependent |
