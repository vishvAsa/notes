The interesting thing about the brutal landscape of Greenland is the number of times it was re-colonized by genetically distinct people starting from as early 2500 BCE! The war-like Scandis +++(=scandinavians)+++ had a tough time dealing with the whale-killing Inuits. We would posit that genetic factors allowed to Inuit coming from Alaska to ultimately triumph.
