## Boogeymen
Source: [TW](https://x.com/blog_supplement/status/2066696627780284914)

Before the hAdi shUlapuruSha came along the go-to metaphors for the mahAmlechCha were King George III and even higher than him was the "Pharaoh".  The latter was foundational to mahAmlechCha identity and was to foreshadow a lot of what was to come.
