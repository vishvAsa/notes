Hence, when trouble comes, H were not able to cohesively respond in a way that helps their interests.

Given the pichChiluNga’s first term, it was a reasonable hedge to go with him as opposed to mahAhAsA, who definitely had a big anti-H agenda on her pakSha. More those who read the ground more closely realized that the luNga was likely to win.
