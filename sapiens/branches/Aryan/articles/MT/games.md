## Ball bat
Source: [TW](https://x.com/blog_supplement/status/2073106205036142697)

The Germans and Dutch play Schlagball, a homolog of cricket; there, the batsman throws up the ball himself and then slogs it. This might indicate a deeper homology with gulikA-krIDA (~gilli-daNDa). Probably the pANDu-s and kaurava-s were playing this game when their ball fell into a well.
