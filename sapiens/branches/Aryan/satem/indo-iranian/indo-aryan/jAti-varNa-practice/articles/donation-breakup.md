**Epigraphic Record of Religious Donations (5th–12th centuries AD / 400s–1100s AD)**  
*(Based on Alexis Sanderson, 2013)*

### Epigraphia Indica (1888–1977)
- **Non-Sectarian**: 41%
- **Śaiva**: 37%
  - Devī: 4%
  - Skanda: 0.5%
- **Vaiṣṇava**: 10%
- **Jaina**: 6.5%
- **Bauddha**: 3.5%
- **Saura**: 2%

### Indian Antiquity (1964–71)
- **Non-Sectarian**: 41%
- **Śaiva**: 33%
  - Devī: 0.8%
- **Vaiṣṇava**: 7.5%
- **Jaina**: 9.5%
- **Bauddha**: 8.1%
- **Saura**: 1%

**Note**: Non-sectarian donations were typically Brahmadeya land grants awarded to Brāhmaṇas without regard to specific sect.  
*Source: Inscriptions published in the mentioned journals.*
