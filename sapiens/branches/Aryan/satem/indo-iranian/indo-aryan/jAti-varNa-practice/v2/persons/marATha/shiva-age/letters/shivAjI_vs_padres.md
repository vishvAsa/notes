## En
Shivaji, deeply angered by this harsh policy, invaded the area of Bardez, not far from Goa. There, he cut off the heads of four priests who refused to convert to his own faith — that is, to become Maratha-Hindus. The priests had been urging the destruction of everyone who did not share their beliefs.  

This act so terrified the Portuguese Viceroy that he was forced to withdraw his severe and oppressive edict. Shivaji then burned and devastated the surrounding countryside and carried away 150 lakh (15 million) pagodas (a large sum of money).
