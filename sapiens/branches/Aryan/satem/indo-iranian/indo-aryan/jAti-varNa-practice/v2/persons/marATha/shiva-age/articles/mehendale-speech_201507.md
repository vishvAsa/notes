**Modern English Translation:**

The remaining buildings, constructed with many stories of square-cut facing stones, along with the numerous mosques of the same material, have lost little of their ancient splendor. They are all equipped with beautiful water tanks, around which stand costly tombs with their own separate chapels or mosques.

**In these places, the Mullahs formerly received generous pensions to pray for the souls of the departed**—a practice they maintain as effective to this day. This is why they eagerly seek funerals in the most prominent locations. The pleasant summer houses overlooking these areas make the spots even more attractive, causing the unemployed townspeople to waste their days in these inviting places.
