Ayaṃ śabdaḥ upayuñjate sma taiḥ: 'satyasya nikaṭavartī bhāṣyaṃ kim' iti.
Kutaḥ nikaṭavartī iti cet, sākṣāt satyasya pratipādanaṃ śabdaiḥ na kartuṃ śakyam.
Satyaṃ tu kevalaṃ tapogamyam. 'tapasā brahma vijijñāsasva' ityeva.

Parantu sarvamapi tapasā eva na vaktuṃ śakyaṃ śabdaḥ api āvaśyako bhavati.
Śabdavinyāsaḥ ekaikarītyā ekaikaiḥ ācāryaiḥ kṛtaḥ.

Viśiṣṭādvaitasiddhāntānuyāyī cet sa vadati viśiṣṭādvaitaparaṃ rāmānujabhāṣyam eva samīcīnaṃ itarat sarvaṃ na yujyate iti.

Iti evaṃ rītyā adhyayanaṃ taiḥ ṣaṣṭivarṣaparyantaṃ svajīvane kṛtam.

Sādhāraṇatyā idānīntana-śāstragrantheṣu kiṃ kriyate? Jñānaṃ vijñānam iti paryāyah iti kriyate.
Jñānaṃ vijñānam iti paryāyatvena svīkṛtya vyākhyānādikaṃ kriyate.

Iti ekaikasya viṣaye'pi bahu bahu vaktavyaṃ vartate. Ācāryaiḥ bahu tatra saṃśodhanaṃ kṛtam.

Idānīntanapaṇḍitaiḥ sarvaiḥ kiṃ cintyate iti cet... etādṛśaviṣaye cintā eva na kriyate sa vicāraḥ anyaḥ.

Sarvair etaiḥ sarvair asmābhiḥ prāyaḥ śrutā iyaṃ śrutiḥ:

Iti ātmadarśanaṃ sampādanīyam. Ātmadarśanasampādanāya vidyamānaṃ sādhanatrayaṃ śravaṇa-manana-nididhyāsanānīti.

Atra draṣṭavyaḥ ityatrāpi sannanta-prayogo nāsti.

Kutaḥ iti cet dhyānaṃ na bhavadadhīnam.

Ataḥ nididhyāsitavyaḥ, dhyātavyaḥ iti anuktvā nididhyāsitavyaḥ iti uktam.

Ataḥ śrutau nididhyāsitavyaḥ iti sannantapratyayeṇa sākaṃ nirdeśaḥ kṛto vartate.

Kutaḥ iti cet nididhyāsitavyaḥ iti itarāṃ dhyātavyaḥ iti evaṃ vyākhyānaṃ kṛtam.
Saṃskṛtavyākaraṇadṛṣṭyā nididhyāsitavyaḥ ityatra ko'rtha?
Iti cet tatra dhyā-dhātoḥ upari sannantaprayogo vartate, sannantaprayogasya upari tavyapratyayasya yojanaṃ vartate.
Ataḥ sannantaprayogasya san-pratyayasya svārasyaṃ kim?
Iti cet: dhyānaṃ na bhavadadhīnam, api tu bhagadadhīnam (bhagavadadhīnam).
Dhyānakaraṇārthaṃ yaḥ prayatnaḥ kartavyaḥ, sa eva kevalaṃ bhavadadhīnaḥ.

Evaṃ nyāyaśāstrasya viṣayo vā bhavatu, vedāntaśāstrasya viṣayo vā bhavatu, kim bahunā, vyākaraṇaśāstrāsthaḥ aneke rahasyāḥ ācāryaiḥ prakaṭītāḥ.
Yathā udāharaṇārthaṃ 'nidhāya hṛdi viśveśam' iti tarkasaṅgraha maṅgalapadyam.
Taiḥ asakṛt ucyate sma: sahasravāraṃ mayā ayaṃ ślokaḥ pāṭhitaḥ, ekaikavāram api nūtana-nūtanavyākhyāḥ mama sphuranti, tat ahaṃ tathaiva vadāmi.
