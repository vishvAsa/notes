Biography written by DR K.C.Narsimhan ,great grand son of Sri Swami.

## Introduction :

Mahamahopadya Kapisthalam Desikachariar Swami of Tirupati was one of the most distinguished exponents of Visishtadavaita school of Philosophy, besides being one of the few great scholars of his time.At the same time he was a ardent devotee of Lord Srinivasa , which made him decline number of remunerative posts and good offers outside Tirupati. Sri Swami was much superior to and deserved greater recognition than what he had received , which itself was quite considerable .He was regarded for several decades as the greatest living authority on Taraka sastra [logic], Nyaya dialetics and Vedantic philosophy during his life time .He had made use of Nyaya in precisely defining the metaphysical categories and epistemology in Visishtadvaita philosophy of Ramanuja .
He was considered as 'Apara Desika avatara'   meaning-  an incarnation of AcharyaVedanta Desika .

## Early life and family.
Sri Desikachariar Swami was born in Trichanur [near Tirupati in Chitoor district of Andrapradesh] on 15 th october ,1855, in Tamil month of Purattasi under the constellation Anuradha star , synchronising with the birthday of Tirumala Nambi--a great savant of Tozhappa family and the maternal uncle of Srimad Ramanuja [the exponent of Visishtadwaita philosophy].

He belonged to Kaushika Gothram like Sri Vedanta Desikar .His father was Sriman Gopalachariar , a scholar and orthodox Vaishnavaite and his mother was Srimathi Venkataraghavaamma.His forefathers might have migrated from the village of Kapisthalam [near Kumbkonam]to Tirupati some two or three generations earlier
He had a younger brother Sriman Rangachari Swami who was equally eminent scholar in Sahitya and Alankara sastras , as he did not stir out of Tirupati , his erudition and scholarship could not be known to all.He was also a distinguished poet.Sri Desikachariar had a sister by name Srimathi Mangammal.
Being poor their father could not afford them the modern education .But by their own efforts ,Sri DesikachariarSwami and his brother studied Sahitya, Alankara ,Vyakarna and Tarka under the able guidance of Sriman Vidwan purisai Sri RangachariarSwami of Tirupati [in the present G.car street]

In his boyhood Sri Desikachariar Swami was noted for his precocious intellect with ingenious grasp of the lessons which made him obtain complete mastery over Vyakarana[grammar] and Tarka[logic] before he was 18 years of age.Then he was intiated into Vedantha[philosopy] and sampradaya works by the famous Sriman LadapuramGopalachariar Swami of Trichanur who was a grihastha intellectual like Sri Vedantha Desika.

When the great sanyasin Srimad Rangaramanuja Mahadesika  Swami of Kethandapatti visited Trichanur during 1874-76,Sri Desikachariar Swami came under the inspiring influence of the yathi and was initiated into esoterics of visishtdwaita  contained in its four classical texts,Sri Bhashya,Gita Bhashya, Rahasyatrayasra,and Bhagvat Vishaya.

Sri Desikachariar Swami married Srimathi Srinivasammaa daughter of Tozhappa family.Through her had two sons  -Sri Kapisthalam Tirumalachariar and Sri Kapisthalam Veeraraghava chariar.Fate was cruel to him as it  snatched away his young wife at his early age leaving his sons motherless.Later he married Srimathi Lakshmamma --a daughter of Arasanapalai family.Through her he had two sons-Sri Kapisthalam Chetlur Gopalachariar and Sri Kapisthlam Chetlur Srinivasachariar and two daughters Srimathi Kamalamma and Srimathi Andalamma.Sri Desikachariar bought a house in Govindrajaswami North Mada street at Tirupati where he lived till he attained paramapada.
When Sri Desika chariar Swami completed his education at the age of twenty , his father wanted him to visit places like Puddukotai , Trivandram etc and participate in Vidwat sadas[discussions,debates].However Sri Swami a very humble person felt that he was too small a man  to engage  eminent pandits in debates.When he told his guru about the desire of his father his guru was pleased about it  encouraged him to fulfill his father's desire and blessed him that he would never experience any defeat or parajaya.
Whilst Sri Desikachariar Swami  was discussing vedantha with his guru,the Kethandatti Swami at Trichanur , both used to be so deeply engrossed in the discussions that they were never conscious of passing of time .It was said that it used to be a pleasure to witness their discussions.

## Head of Sanskrit college at Tirupati.

The persistent efforts of Sri Desikachariar swami resulted in the starting of the Grand Duff Sanskrit Patasala in 1885 with the assistance of the then Mahanth [person administering Tirumala temple] and it was in the fitness of things that  Swami  became the first Head Pandit and in charge of Patasala ,in which capacity he served for many years .Mahanth Sri Prayaga Dasji had high regards for the SriSwami and he often took his advise on religious matters.

A doubt was raised in 1912 about utilising the Devasthanam funds for educational purposes .It was resolved ,when a act was passed in 1913 which made it possible for Devasthanam  Funds to be utilised for educational purposes .At  that time the Patasala was upgraded into a Sanskrit college and Sri Desikachariar Swami ,by virtue of his eminence was made the first Vice Principal and head of the college ,even though he had no English education .In this capacity he continued till 1926 .Thus Sri Swami rendered distinguished service to the institution for over forty years .Later in his life his brother's son Sri Kapisthalam Krishnamachariar was appointed as the Principal as per the university rules.

Sri Desikachariar Swami taught lessons to his pupils in a lucid way .He was affectionate   towards his students and disciples.He used to take Purva Mimasa for Siromani examinations with ease and impressed upon  the students  with his command of the subject although he did not undergo any training in the same under any guru.An Advaitin by name Sri Ottipali Narakanteerava Sastrulu who also a pandit in the Patasala had difference of opinion with Sri Swami on a particular subject and finally they reconciled .



## Invitations and visits :

Sri Desikachariar Swami's merits were recognised in all the Indian states of South India and more than once in far off Benares [kasi] and other places in Northern India.
At the age of 27 he was invited by the Maharaja of Travancore to participate in the Vedantic discussions held in the traditional style during Navarathri celeberations  in his palace.There Sri Swami established the truths of Visishtadvaita by refuting the argumentsof rival schools of thought and the Maharaja was all praise for his subtle intellect, vast learning and dialectic skill and suitably honoured Sri Swami.This was the first experience of Sri Swami to be involved in Vedantic Sadas outside Tirupati.

In 1892 Sir S.Subramania aiyar,the distinguished judge of Madras high court sought Sri Swami's help to learn the Sankara Bhashyam and Advaitic system of Philosophy and Sri Aiyer expressed his admiration for Swami's unique powers of intuiting the fundamental and the foundational tenets of each system of Philosophy and expressing them with unsurpassed lucidity.

In 1912 Sri Swamy was invited to Benares for the first time by Sri Sathya Theertha Swami of Uttaradhi Mutt who was one of those propogating Madhwa philosophy , to participate in a Vidwat sadas .Sri Desikachariar Swami's lectures made an astounding impression on the sadas and the Uttaradhi Mutt Swami was so impressed with the knowledge of Sri Desikachariar Swamy  that he not only honoured him at the sadas  but also invited him for all the Vidwat Sadas conducted by him and suitably honoured Sri Desikachariar Swami by giving the first place to him.Particularily ,during all the visits of Uttaradhi Mutt Swami to Trichanur ,he never failed to invite Sri Desikachariar Swami and honour him .Hence it became a regular feature for Sri Desikachariar Swami to participate in every annual Vidwat Sadas conducted by Uttaradhi Mutt Swami  at various places like Rajamundry, Raichur, Gadag, Bhagalkot ,Belgaum, Bangalore etc.Sri Uttaradhi Swami took the help of Sri Desikachariar Swami ,during his visit to Belgaum ,for justifying the sentence " Maya vada  macchastram pracchanam baudda muaccyate"in a book written by Sri Lakshmi puram Srinivasacharyulu ,the astana poet of Mysore palace ,when the other pandits and poets from Mysore state opposed the same.
Sri DesikachariarSwami visited Benares in 1911 and 1918 thrice at the invitation of distinguished men and by his supreme dialectic skill in Vedantic disputations , prodiguos memory  and unassailable logic, sustained the claim of Visishtadvaita to be the only system that satisfied the tests of Scriptures and Reason.
Pandit Madan Mohan Malavia ,the then Vice chancellor of Benares Hindu University was so fascinated by Sri Swami's intellect ,that he offered him a post in the University,But Sri Swami did not accept it as he did not want to leave Tirupati--the abode of Lord Srinivasa .Neverthless Sri Malvya had a very  high regard and reverence for Sri Swami .Sri Malaviya paid a visit to Sri Swami's   residence at Tirupati where they conversed in sanskrit for few hours .

One of Sri Swami's visits to Benares was at the invitation of Sri Jagathguru Gadi Ananthachariar Swami .While Sri Anantha chariar Swami was at Benares ,the Advaitins challenged him and objected to his calling himself 'Jagadguru'.Since he could not  refute the challenge a telegram was sent to Sri Desikachariar Swami requesting him to immediately proceed to Benares and meet the challenge of Advaitins by expounding the tenets of Visishtadvaita philosophy.After Swami reached Benares ,the polemics seem to have been conducted in the form of court room trial with the Maharaja of Benares acting as judge.The Advaitins were effectively defeated which resulted in conceding their defeat in writing and also that Sriman Desikachariar Swamy can claim  the tittle as the legitimate Jagadguru.At this ,Sriman Desikachariar convinced them to agree for the continuance of the Jagadguru title claimed by Sriman Gadi Ananthachariar Swami,since Sriman Desikachariar Swami was not after titles..

In the year 1912 ,Sriman Swami was invited by the Maharaja of Pithapuram to be his Astahana Vidwan.But as Sriman Swami never wanted to leave lord Srinivasa's sannidhi ,he declined the offer.He however attended the Navarathri celeberations at Pithapuram and delivered lectures  for which he was profusely honoured by the Maharaja.Where ever Sriman Swami went to participate in the discussions ,he made an ever lasting impression on the pandits with his lucid and awe inspiring expositions .

During 1930 Sriman Swami was invited by Sri Suvarathindra Swamiji of Sri Raghvendra Swami Mutt in the Vidwat Sadas held at Kumbakonam where eminent Pandits like Sri Virupaksha Sastrulu of Mysore and others were present.At that time he delivered a lecture which was appreciated by Sri Suvarithindra Swamiji ,who honoured Sriman Desikachariar Swami  with a shawl and  a silver vessel full of rupee coins.

After his retirement as Vice Principal of the Sanskrit college ,SriSwami was made the Devasthanam Poet Laurete [Astana Vidwan]thanks to the generosity of Sri Mahant Prayaga Dasji ,whose cultural discernment was keen enough to recognise the erudition of Sri Swami.Fortunately he was given the same amount  as his former salary as honorarium /pension for the rest of his life.It was this leisure so kindly afforded to him that had enabled him to produce some valuable books which none in the country but he could have written .

.During 1927-28 Sri Desikachariar Swami was invited by the Maharaja of Rewa to be the Astana Vidwan of his state .But the Mahant was not prepared to spare the services of Sri Swami nor did Sri swami want to leave Tirupati and be away from Lord Srinivasa .

When the  Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanam committee took over the Administration from the Mahant ,there were some objections from some quarters for the continuation of Sri Swami as the Astana Vidwan of T.T.Devasthanam.Sri Mahant who was the president of the committee was very particular about continuing with Sri
Thus Sriman Swami had the unique distinction of being the first Astana Vidwan of T.T.Devasthanam after the advent of the committee .Evidently it was the wish of Lord Srinivasa to retain his ardent devotee with himself .
In 1927 he delivered lectures on Vedantha -about fifty in number before a distinguished and appreciative audience in Vedanta Desika temple at Mylapore.

During 1927 -31 Sriman Desikachariar Swami presided over a number of Vaishnava conferences in Poonamallee , Tiruvaheendrapuram ,Kanchepuram,Mannargudi and other places.He was one of the special invitees of Sri Vaishnava Sidhantha  sabha at Mannargudi  where Sriman Bhagvathi Shyam Chetlur Narsimhachariar presided .Sri Desikachariar Swami expounded Visishtadvaita  Sidhantha there to the delight of all the discerning and perceptive audience .
At one of the Vaishnava sabhas held at Tiruvaheendrapuram under the presidentship of Sriman Desikachariar Swami,Sriman Bhagavatvishayam Chetlur NarsimhachariarSwami pointed out that Lord Devanatha [the presiding deity at Tiruvaheendrapuram temple and the place where in  Sri Desikan had stayed for a number of years ] was capable of greater accessibility [sowlabya] than Lord Srinivasa .Sriman Desikachariar Swami in his presidential concluding remarks commented that for Swami Vedanta Desika the ultimate refuge was Lord Srinivasa as is evidenced by his firts sloka in Dayasatakam'Prapadhyetham girim srayaha '.But if Sriman Narsimhachariar wanted he could salute Lord Devanatha as 'Namathaha shri disaanaam naatham.'This reveals Sriman Desikachariars Swami's attachment to Lord Srinivasa which is quite in line with Sri Swami Desikan's own conviction.This wonderful exposition was applauded by the discerning audience that had gathered there.
Sri Desikachariar Swami was invited by the Calcutta university to take the Sanskrit chair but he declined as his attachment to Lord Srinivasa was very strong..

In 1931, Sri Swami delivered the Rukmini lectures instituted by Sri .N.Gopalaswami Ayyangar at Mylapore .There he elucidated the essential principles of Dwaita, Adwaita and Visishtadwaita which was appreciated by the eminent pandits who had gathered there..He took one day to explain Dwaita philosophy ,two days for Advaita philosophy and finally two days for Visishtadwaita philosophy.
After hearing his exposition on Adavita philosophy ,it appeared that everybody was wondering ,how he  could establish the primacy of Visishtadwaita.But he sustained the claim that  of Visishtadwaita to be the only system that satisfied tests of scriptures  and Reason.
Sri P.N.Srinivasachariar ,the then principal of Pachaiyappa college who attended the lectures stated that the logical and lucid style and genius exhibited by Sri Swami in his comparison of the three systems was a marvel of metaphysical exposition .
His intellectual vigour and zest with which he threw himself into the discussions of different schools of Vedantic thought were never impaired by age , physical infirmities or failing eyesight.

The Rukmini lectures of Sri Swamy were published as Traya -sangraha in Tamil.This was translated into English by Sri M.R.Rajagopal Ayyangar of Madras formerly of Education service , an erudite scholar with a profound knowledge of philosophical doctrines  and in Telugu by Vangipuram Sri Rangaswami Ayyangar of Trichanur ,who is a author of many books in Telugu, Tamil and English and was one of the disciples of Sri Desikachariar Swami.

Sri Desikachariar Swami showed his genius by giving 10 meanings for the slokam in Ramayana :

'Ramam dasradham viddhi, maam viddhi janakatmajaaam,
Ayodyam ataveem viddhi, gaccha thatha yada sukam'

in such way that the gist of entire Ramayana is brought out .Just as Sri Valmiki taught Ramayana to Lavakusa , Sri Swami taught this to his disciple Sri Susvaram Gopalakrishnacharyulu who has popularised the interpretation.

3.Sri SarikaAdhikara ratnamala--
This work is in sanskrit  .This is the fruit of more than 60 yearsof philosophcal study and his constant and deep devotion to Srinivasar
This is based on Ramanujas Sri Bhashya which interprets sutras .
He introduces every sutra with a preliminary objection , comments on it and explains how the sutra meets to objection He states the sutra and gives its meaning Then he writes his sloka in praise of Srinivasar by adding  the quality of Brahman deduced from  that  sutra.This followed by the  author's explanation of the verse The section than ends with purvapaksha and siddhantha ,each in a short paragraph.
Srinivasa Raghavan of Puddukotai who edited this book says the above .There are 156 such adikarans and one or two slokas for each sutra numbering 188 in which 156 qualities are predicted of Lord Srinivasa .

This work establishes the devotional identity of Narayana with Lord Srinivasa .

Note this book was also published recently by Srirangam Srimad Andavan Ashram on the eve of Sri Andavan's 80th birthday on 21.5.2015.

Kapisthalam Desikachariar Swamy---Awards and Distinctions.
On 1st January 1917,the tittle of "Mahamahopadyaya' was conferred on Sri Desikachariar Swamy by the government for his outstanding contribution to sanskrit ,an honour not easily bestowed in those days.Sri C Doraiswami Ayengar , a leading advocate of Chitoor [Andra Pradesh] but native of Tirupathi ,an admirer of Sri Swamy and a member of the central Legislative Assembly took a lot of interest in it and helped in getting the tittle conferred on Sri Swamy by the government.

In those days of British imperialism ,the visit of Prince of Wales to Madras was unique and in January 1922 it was the first Royal family visit.As such it had a grandeur and significance ,unparalleled .At that time Sri Desikichariar Swami was the First to be introduced to him as the foremost  of the orthodox hierarchy and he was one of the two Sanskrit scholars to be rewarded by the royalty with a Thoda for literary eminence in Sanskrit on 13 th January 1922 at the Senate house.

At a large gathering of distinguished pandits like Sri Navalapakkam Narashimhachariar Swami, Sri Ayya Krishnamachariar Swami of Thathacharya family, Jagadguru Sri Gadi Ananthachariar Swami, Tirukotiyur Swami, Sriman A.V.Gopalachariar,U.V.Srinivasa Ayyengar,and several other eminent scholars of the calibre of Diggajas and distinguished persons from all parts of India  at Kanchipuram, the tittle of Sri Ramanuja Siddhantha Vijaya Dwajam 'was conferred upon Sri Desikachariar Swami , the sanad was kept in a silver casket mounted on two elephants carrying a Vijaya dwajam [flag mast].It is significant that this function was held at ahobila mutt at Kanchipuram.Sriman U.Ve Madurantakam T.E.Veerararaghava cahriar Swamy one of the greatest living exponents of Visisthadvaita , was present at the occasion .
Sri Desika chariar was the recepient of the honorary degree of 'Kala Prapurna'--equivalent to doctrate degree  by the Andra university on 13 th March 1937 at a special convocation where Sri Pingali Lakshmi kantam ,the university orator in Telugu had the honour of reading the citation of Sri Swami .[The English translation is given below]
Sri P.Lakshmi kantam , the University Orator  in Telugu requested the Pro Chancellor in the following speech, to confer the honoraray Doctrate Degree of Kala Prapurna in absentia upon Mahamahopadyaya Kapisthalam Desikachariaryulu:

Respected Pro Chancellor of the university,

I humbly submit that the honorary degree of Kalaprapurna ,earlier resolved by the Senate ,be conferred upon Ramanuja Siddhantha Vijayadwaja , Mahamahopadyaya Sriman Kapisthalam Desikachariar Swami , in recognition of his contribution to Indian thought , on behalf of the University Senate.
Sri Desikachariar , a jewel among scholars is an Andra Gautama in logic[tarka]. another Ramanuja in Vedanta [philosophy] and a flag bearer holding aloft the Visishtadvaita banner , as enunciated by Ramanuja.
Even at a early age  ,he won recognition for his scholarship from many eminent scholars in the court of Maharaja of Travancore .
His deep commitment  to Visishtadvaita has not percluded him from mastering and expounding Dwaita and Advaita  with equal felicity and perspicacity , compelling the esteem and reverence of all schools of Indian philosophical thought by his sheer intellectual brilliance and honesty, unassailable and irrefutable logic, and clarity of vision and exposition .His thorough understanding of all systems of Philosophy is a proof of his unbiased and genuine quest for truth, totally devoid of sectarian aberrations .

Sri Swamy's views were highly valued  and sought by the scholars of all the three systems on many knotty and controversial points if interpretation ,and he has thus become the last word in Indian philosophy.No wonder Pandit Madan mohan Malviayaji  the greatest upholder  of Bharatiya culture regarded Sri Swami as his preceptor.

The award of the tittle , Mahamahopadyaya by the Government of India is a recognition of Sri Swami's erudition .
This university deems it as its duty to honour this human embodiment of the goddess of learning  by awarding the tittle' Kalaprapurna 'and i therefore supplicate that the tittle be conferred on Sri DesikachariarSwamy.

The Pro Chancellor in conferring the Degree said --
" In the name and on behalf of the senate of the Andra pradesh University ,i confer upon MahamahopadyayaDesikacharyulu the Honorary Doctorate Degree of Kalapprapurna in absentia  and in token thereof permit the Registrar to present him with his diploma and i authorise him to wear these Khillats prescribed as academic robes as the insignia of his degree."

Sri Desikachariar Swami was the examiner for Oriental tittle examination of Madras University for three years.Then he was the examiner in Mysore and Benares Hindu Universities.He was also a member of   Board of Studies of Sanskrit of Benares University and later chairman of the board for three years .

He had very close contacts with Pandit Madan Mohan Malavia , who in turn had a high regard and reverence for Sri Swami for his scholarship.
