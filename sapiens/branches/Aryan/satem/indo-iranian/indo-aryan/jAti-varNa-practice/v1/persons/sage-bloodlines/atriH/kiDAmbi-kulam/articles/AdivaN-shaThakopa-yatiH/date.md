Adivan satakopan date is traditionally held to be 1378-1458 or thereabout. But we may have to move his date to 1430s-1500 or thereabout based in inscriptional evidence. His period should have started about 1458.. 60 yrs (one samvatsara cycle) later than the traditional dates claimed by the matha.

Ahobila Matha's (US run) website cites the 1555 inscription of Shashta Parankusha swamy (6th Pontiff) but the Guruparampara published says swamy entered the cave in Ahobilam at 1513 never to return.

That shall also explain how allasani peddanna (who was in krishnarayas durbar) calls him as his acharya among other things - like inscriptions of his successor from 1515 or so CE. Peddanna refers to AVS in manu caritamu and it was completed much before this date (under Krishnadevarayas reign).

AVS date in 1400s matches well with annamacharya. There is no problem.

Sixth AS is claimed to have been felicitated by mukunda deva gajapati who ruled from 1560-68 only.
