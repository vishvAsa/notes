Similarly manavala mamuni is held to have lived from 1370-1443. 

But thiruvaimozhipillai is claimed to have lived in madurai before mamunis birth and served as minister to pandyas. This is impossible given that it was sultanate rule from 1323 to 1371. Otoh, vijayanagar regents ruled in place of pandyan minor princes in early 1400s and thiruvaimozhipillai story matches with that date only. 

Periya thirumudi adaivu was written by son of Mamuni's sishya. Now this text refers to people who lived in 1570s. So holding Mamuni to have lived till 1443 only sounds impossible.

The birth dates of Adivan-shatakopa swamy and Manavalamamuni both should be brought to 1400s from their birthday in 1370-80 to reconcile the inscriptional evidences we find during the time of each and later day pontiffs of the former.
