## Gopana inscription
Gopana himself claims that he was a sishya of VD in his work sindhumati vilasa. Last year of VD as per tradition is 1369. Gopanarya inscription at Srirangam is dated 1371. But tradition holds that when Gopana reconquered Srirangam, VD wrote the Slokas which are inscribed in the 1371 inscription. 

Srirangam was most probably reconquered by early 1360s since there is an inscription dated 1359 south of Srirangam in Pudukkottai district which refers to kumara kampanas victories in the region. Inscription must have been made when the temple was properly restored which might have taken a few years.
