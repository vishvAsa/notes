## Date
Now prativadi bhayankaram swami (the original one) is claimed to have learned sri bhashyam from Kumara varada (son of Desika) and is also stated that he was a sishya of mamuni. He was also given samashrayana by kumara varada only. Now kumara varada lived till 1415-20. PB swamy should have been born in the 1390s then.

## vaDakalai
was it the original Annan or his son/grandson who became tk? Latter is more possible. 

Also, the title PB originally belonged to Swami Desikan as seen from the colophon of sindhumativilasa written by Gopanarya, Swami's sishya. So KV gave the title of his father to PB swami.
