The sect met with considerable military success in Arabia and Persia, and at one time threatened to spread over the Islamic world. The following is an account of the taking of Mecca by Saud, the grandson of the founder, in 1803: ‚ÄúThe sanctity of the place subdued the barbarous spirit of the conquerors, and not the slightest excesses were committed against the people.
