## Tāj Begum
Tāj Begum, who was a Mughal princess of Persian background, converted into Vaishnavism.

She wrote:  

> "Listen, beloved of my heart, to the story of my heart—  
I will endure slander, I will bear disgrace for you.  
I had resolved to worship the Allah, yet I will forget my prayers (namaz),  
I will abandon the kalma and the Quran, and embrace all virtues for you.
>
> O dark and handsome crown of all beauty,  
In the burning mark of your love, I will willingly burn myself away.  
For the son of Nanda, I offer myself completely at your form  
Though born a Turk, I will live as a Hindu for you."
