## Cohesion
Japan's historical & demographic core (Kanto/Tokyo, Kansai/Osaka-Kyoto, Chugoku/Hiroshima on Honshu's Pacific/Seto coast) holds ~58-60% of pop today (Kanto ~34-35%, Kansai ~16%, Chugoku ~6%). Edo era: even higher central Honshu focus (~80% est. on main plains). Shared Japanese language & culture since Yamato period (~1500+ yrs). Core zones total ~97k km² (< Telangana 112k km²); full spread ~378k km² (near MP 308k). Mountainous islands + isolation enabled unity & homogeneity.
