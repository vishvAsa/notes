Source: [TW](https://x.com/blog_supplement/status/2044929006659944869)

With nations as with mere mortals: Reaching for the high mark, one performs great deeds, but stumbling, barely misses it and falls to the ground like emperor yayAti on his downward course. There, some quietly pass into the soil of time like a once coursing dead horse on the steppe. But others, after their fall, are chosen for a special punishment by the victors – a forceful erasure that doesn't even spare their bones whitening on the sands of time. mlechCha-s tend to be such in victory, and a mortal or a nation should be utterly wary of that.
