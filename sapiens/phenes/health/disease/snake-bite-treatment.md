+++
title = "Snake bite treatment"
+++

Move to safety: Ensure the snake is no longer a threat. Do not attempt to catch or kill it.  
Call emergency services: In India, dial 108 or 112 for immediate help.  
Keep calm and still: Movement accelerates venom spread. Have the person lie down.  
Immobilize the limb: Use a splint or sling; keep the bite at or slightly below heart level.  
Remove constricting items: Rings, watches, or tight clothing should be taken off before swelling begins.  
Cover the wound: Use a clean, dry dressing. Do not apply ointments, ice, or chemicals.  
Monitor symptoms: Watch for breathing difficulty, drooping eyelids, confusion, severe swelling, or bleeding.  
CPR if unresponsive: Begin if trained, until medical help arrives.  
Self transport to hospital with immobilization in case of delay.
