On the origin of syphilis.  The DNA evidence shows it originated in the Americas, but you could have figured it out without that DNA evidence. 

Once it showed  up ( Italy around 1494) it spreads very rapidly. In Japan by 1520. This explosive speed means that the time window for it coming into existence in the Old World is only \~25 years deep. The time window for it appearing in the  New World is thousands of years deep.
