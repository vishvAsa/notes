China has built far more housing and infrastructure than needed and far more factories than needed for her own consumption. China has overworked her people & as a result has destroyed her demographics. 

India needs more factories, housing & infrastructure but needs balance. India must aim for balance between imports and exports and there is no sense trying to become the "factory for the world" like China.
