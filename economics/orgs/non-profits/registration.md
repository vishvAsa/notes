Historically, trusts were registered under Section 12AA. The government later updated the law, requiring all trusts to migrate to or register under **Section 12AB**. 

It is **Form No. 10AD**, which is the formal order granting registration to a trust under **Section 12AB** of the Income Tax Act, 1961. This document confirms that the trust has successfully secured this crucial registration.
