## C

*To Sister Nivedita*

14, GREYCOAT GARDENS  
WESTMINSTER

*October 29, 1896*

DEAR MISS \[MARGARET\] NOBLE —

I will be at yours on Friday next, at 4 p.m.

I did not know of any arrangements made to meet anybody Friday last,
hence my absence.

Yours,  
VIVEKANANDA
