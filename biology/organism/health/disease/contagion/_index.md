## साङ्क्रामिकरोगाः
प्रसङ्गाद् गात्रसंस्पर्शान् निश्वासात् सहभोजनात्।  
सहशय्यासनाच् चापि वस्त्रमाल्यानुलेपनात्॥  
कुष्ठं ज्वरश्च शोषश्च नेत्राभिष्यन्द एव च।  
औपसर्गिकरोगाश्च संक्रामन्ति नरान् नरम्॥ ३

## Gender difference
### Anti-viral
- 1) Several immune regulating genes are located on X Chromosome.
- 2) Men tend to have high inflammatory mediators
- 3) Women are good in producing anti viral mediators called Interferon 
- 4) The T lymphocytes which forms the big defence against the virus are recruited efficiently and early in women.
