## Constituents
- High-pressure decorative laminates (HPDLs) such as Formica or Wilsonart are generally comprised of layers of wood fibers, decorative paper (virgin and recycled), and (melamine) resin (a petrochemical product) glossy protective layer.
- Fabricating and installing can now be done with low-VOC adhesives.
