## Comment handling
### Best practice 
- For controvertial videos - hold and moderate comments. Don't platform abusive and spammy commenters.
- Collate and answer comments at the top level.
- Post a link to this comment as a response to the triggering comment; or just say - "Answered as a top level comment".

### Problems
- When a (abusive/ spammy?) commentor is hidden or his post deleted, response is not hidden.
- By default YT only shows "Top" comments, and not "all" comments (which the viewer has to manually select.)
- Deleting a repeated comment was observed to delete an unintended comment 20260402.

### Review
YT studio has a good interface for handling comments.  
Useful filters 

- Comments per video
- Published, sort by newest
- Held, sort by newest

### Moderation 

- Can automatically hold all comments. Can make this default

When moderation is enabled, commenters are confused when their comments don't appear immediately, and mistakenly think that they are deleted. Then they paste the same comment repeatedly.
