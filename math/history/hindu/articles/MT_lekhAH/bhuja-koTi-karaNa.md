It is wrong to call the theorem of the Right Triangle as baudhAyana's theorem, just as it is wrong to call it the Pythagorean theorem. It was not discovered first by baudhAyana. He was merely stating what was known to the adhvaryu-s before him, who constructed the vedi-s specified in the yajurveda. The current H term for the theorem is the bhujA-koTi-karNa-nyAya. A good descriptive term that can be abbreviated to bh-k-k for use in school.
