## USA advantage

I have often wondered about the below. I have arrived a list of reasons whose individual weights might be different for different people. There may several reasons beyond these:

### Hub
First and foremost, academic pursuits are a social venture as human are social primates. Hence, being part of a mafia/cartel, being at the center of where the cartel is active is important for these pursuits. That center and hive of activity is currently the USA.

### Money
Academic pursuits need money. The USA is effectively the richest nation and spends some of its riches to fund these pursuits. India cannot match that.

### Merit
Until recently, there was a window of meritocratic operation in the US. In that window people were objectively employed based on merit rather than other criteria. That is eroding currently but its afterglow is still there. Unfortunately, India not too meritocratic.

### Family
The above also meant better opportunities for family. Further, being a country with a low population density many places offer opportunities to experience the outdoors, clean air, water and safety from street dogs. India falls short in these metrics.

### Bureaucracy
The degree of bureaucracy for certain things is lower making life easier.

### Safety
Being a continent on the other side of the world it is relatively safe from invaders who can disrupt civil society.

### American ID
Some Indians internally feel very American and identify with it at a deep level even when they were in India. Hence, for them it is actually a homecoming. 

On the other hand, many Hindus feel more at home in India. They would gladly relocate if India would offer some of the above in a serious way.
