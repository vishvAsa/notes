## Galdikas
Source: [TW](https://x.com/blog_supplement/status/2037003009675120659)

I learnt today that Birutė Galdikas, the primatologist who devoted her life to the study of the great red ape, has passed away. By some strange coincidence, I was thinking of her and the orang lineage just a couple of days ago. Louis Leakey, the founder of the famous Leakey dynasty of anthropologists, sent three women, Goodall, Fossey and Galdikas to study the chimp, the gorilla and the orangutan. Their work contributed greatly to understanding ourselves.
