+Research

What's in this page?

Motivations

  • Compare with motivation for kAvya.
  • It’s a respectable vocation.
  • Appreciation of the best minds of the field/ tradition (not necessarily contemporary).
  • Better personal clarity. This is necessary and sufficient for acquiring background knowledge, but not for deep research (see “Practicing western science” article).

Preparation

  • It’s important to have some solid background knowledge about the one’s field (and progressively less in surrounding maNDala-s).
  • Schoolboy conception of science overemphasises preference (see “Practicing western science” article).
