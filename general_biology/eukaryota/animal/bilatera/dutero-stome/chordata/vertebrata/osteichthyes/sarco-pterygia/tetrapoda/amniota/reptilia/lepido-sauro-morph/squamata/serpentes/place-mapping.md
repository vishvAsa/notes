## Handling tips
- Do not attempt to handle snakes — even non‑venomous ones may bite defensively.
- Call local rescuers if a snake enters your home; killing snakes is illegal under the Wildlife Protection Act.
- Keep surroundings clean (remove garbage, rodent habitats) to reduce snake visits.
- Be cautious during monsoon — snakes are more active when burrows flood.
