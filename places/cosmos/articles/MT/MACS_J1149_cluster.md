The glorious MACS J1149.5+2223 cluster with 100s of galaxies visualized by JWST. When the light from this cluster at 5 billion light-years away started on its journey towards us the sun and solar system were not yet born. How many types of life and civilization have emerged and vanished among these 100s of galaxies dominated by a central elliptical will probably remain entirely unknown to us. The very existence of this cluster was unknown till the 1990s, and about 3-4 decades later, we have this stupendously clear image of it. One needs to repeatedly gaze at such images, as it simultaneously makes existence feel utterly meaningless or so deeply meaningful that we can barely grasp it.
