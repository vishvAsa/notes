"binivAle", the karhaDe brAhmaNa, whose troops stood at the front in mArATha lines, led the successful effort to restore marATha empire in the north after the pAnipaT battle, extracted revenge (and tribute) from the rohilla-s, breaking the tomb of najib khan. peshva mAdhava rAv was so pleased that he had golden flowers showered on him as he entered puNe.
